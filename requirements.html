Driving Record (MVR)

Motor Vehicle Record Standards

Your MVR tells us about your driving history. Here's exactly what we're looking at:

5-Year Major Violations

No major violations in the last 5 years. This includes reckless driving, hit-and-run, and driving under suspension.

1-Year Moving Violations

Maximum 2 moving violations (tickets) in the last 12 months.

3-Year Accident History

Maximum 1 at-fault accident in the last 3 years.
